## ⚸ Black Moon Lilith

Feminine religious figure. Primordial she-demon banished from Eden for disobeying Adam. Often represented by the Serpent.

## ♰ Western Syriac Cross

Used to denote New Testament and Christian references.

## ☩ Hebrew Cross

Used to denote Old Testament and Hebrew Bible references.

## ☦ Ankh

General ancient mythology and syncretic religious elements.

## ℇ Euler's Constant

Represents collective unconscious and grimoire traditions.

## ש Shin (Hebrew)

Symbolizes divine fire, spiritual energy, and transformation in Kabbalah.

## ☣ Biohazard

Marks content involving corruption, contamination, or forbidden knowledge.

## ⚥ Hermaphrodite

Dual-natured or androgynous divine figures.

## ⌱ Dimension Origin

Marks content about dimensional boundaries, seals, and cosmic structures.

## ⛧ Inverted Pentagram

Demonic or infernal references. Lesser Key of Solomon content.
